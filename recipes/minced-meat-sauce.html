Minced Meat Sauce

This is what home tastes like.

Great recipe for beginners! Here's a tip: this goes great with mashed potatoes

20 minutes and serves 4 people

Here's What You Need:

  • 400g Minced Meat
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 tbsp Wheat Flours
  • 1 Bouillon Cube
  • 2 dl Water
  • 2 dl Cooking Cream
  • Whole 6-8 Allspice
  • ½ tsp Black Pepper
  • ½ dl Chopped Parsley

And How It's Done:

  • Sauté the minced meat and chopped onion in a small amount of oil. Sprinkle in the wheat flour. Fry for few minutes.
  • Crumble the bouillon cube on top. Add water, allspice and black pepper. Mix evenly and bring to a boil.
  • Add cooking cream. Let boil for 5 minutes.
  • Optional: finalize with chopped parsley.
